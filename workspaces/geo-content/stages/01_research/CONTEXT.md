## Process
From the target query, produce a research brief:
- The **primary answer**: 2–3 sentences that directly answer the query (draws only on business-facts).
- **Questions travellers ask** around this query (candidate FAQ items).
- **Angles / subtopics** worth covering, and which facts support each.
- Notes on competing/adjacent info the page should address.

Do not write final copy here — this is direction-setting.
