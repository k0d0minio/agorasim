# Brand — Layer 3 reference

- **Logo**: circular badge — a classic car over layered Saloia hills at sunset, "Agorasim" script,
  "Countryside Experiences / Events". File: `web/public/images/logo.png`.
- **Palette** (warm Saloia earth tones + countryside green; defined in
  `web/src/app/globals.css`):
  - Background: warm cream. Foreground: deep warm brown.
  - Primary/accent: forest/olive **green** (CTAs, links).
  - Secondary: sand. Supporting: terracotta / rust (from the logo hills).
- **Type**: headings in **Fraunces** (serif, editorial); body in **Geist** (sans).
- **Imagery**: golden-hour lifestyle photography — classic cars, food spreads, vineyards, villages,
  people. Real assets in `web/public/images/`.
- **Feel**: editorial, unhurried, tactile. Generous whitespace, large imagery, calm typography.
